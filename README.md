# Unified Biology

**Joseph Shields** · 2026

---

The three natures of space are not properties of physics alone. They describe
how any system must relate to its environment if it is to exist at all. In
biological systems they appear with striking precision — not as metaphors, but
as identifiable structures performing identifiable functions.

This repository applies the Unified Mechanics framework to biology, neuroscience,
consciousness, and psychiatry.

| Nature | Channel | Biological Structure | Function |
|---|---|---|---|
| Acknowledgement | Boundary | Synapse · Insular cortex | The gap where information crosses; the body reporting its own state |
| Exploration | Light | Action potential · Dopamine | Signal propagating; novelty detected; new territory encoded |
| Familiarity | Matter | Long-term potentiation · Basal ganglia | Synaptic strengthening; habit; automatic behaviour |

**Consciousness** is the insular cortex's real-time map of the organism's
boundary state — not a product of thinking, but the boundary channel reporting
from inside a living system.

**Feelings** are the scaffold between input and output. Not noise. Not side
effects. The boundary negotiation, experienced from the inside.

**The soul** is the unique synaptic weight distribution accumulated through every
boundary crossed, every familiarity formed, every moment of exploration. It
exists nowhere else. It cannot be copied.
